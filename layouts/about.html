About Me

Lifelong tinkerer. Multi-disciplinary maker. Honest about the failures.

My Journey

I've been hands-on my whole life, tinkering since I was a kid. These days that looks like 3D printing, electronics, Arduino and Raspberry Pi projects, and yes — crochet. The through-line isn't the medium, it's the itch to figure things out.

Teddy Spark exists because I love the process: the failed attempts, the unexpected solutions, the moment something finally works. This site is where I document that journey honestly — the good builds and the bad ones — and share it with anyone who's got the same itch.

This site is not about polished influencer hype or corporate tutorials. It's just me, sharing what I make and learn, in a conversational, honest, and practical way.
