So here we are. Teddy Spark is live.

I've been tinkering my whole life — 3D printing, electronics, Arduino projects, Raspberry Pi builds, crochet — and I've been meaning to document it properly for years. This is that documentation.

## What this site is

A place where I put the things I'm working on. Behind-the-build posts, failures included. Project showcases for the things that actually worked. Honest reviews of tools and materials I've tried. Quick tips when I figure out a better way to do something. And project jumps — because yes, I switch projects a lot, and I'm not apologizing for it.

## What this site isn't

It's not a polished YouTube channel with a production team. It's not a sponsored content machine. It's not going to tell you that you NEED this tool in your life or that my projects are REVOLUTIONARY.

It's just me, building stuff, sharing what I learn.

## What's coming

I've got a few builds in progress right now. Check back soon — and follow on YouTube for the latest updates.

Welcome to the workshop. ⚡
